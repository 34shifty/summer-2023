Hi, my name is Nazar

I am 15 years old and I am from Uzhhorod, Ukraine. I love playing basketball and planning on having a successfull career. Also, I am really good at Math and Physics, so programming is something that I felt in love with, easily.

Basketball

I started playing basketball when I was 12. I fell in love with this because it showed a path of becoming a man when I needed it the most. I want to be a professional basketnall player with all my heart. Unfortunately, we have no teams my age in Uzhhorod, so i can't show my skills to coaches, but I am still working and waiting for my time to shine.

Academics

I just finished 9th grade of middle school and will go for 2 more years of highschool in Linguistic Gymnasium named after T. Shevchenko. I am study hard and finished middle school with A-A+ grades. Planning on studying in National Technical University of Ukraine after finishing school.

Programming

I know Math and Physics well, so when I started thinking about my future career, I no-doubt choose programmig. I find it interestin and highly paid job where you can always evolve and learn new things
